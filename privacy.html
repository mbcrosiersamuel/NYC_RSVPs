Privacy Policy

Overview

We collect basic analytics data to understand how visitors use our site. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

Information We Collect

We use PostHog for website analytics. This service collects the following types of information:

  • Pages visited
  • Visitor counts and traffic patterns
  • Technical information (browser type, device type)
  • General geographic location (city/region level, not precise address)

This is standard website analytics data similar to that collected by most websites. We use this information to understand site usage and improve our services.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Precise location data
  • Personally identifiable information
  • Payment information (we do not process payments)
  • Any data beyond basic analytics

Cookies

Our analytics service (PostHog) uses cookies to track visits and usage patterns. These are standard analytics cookies used to count visitors and analyze site usage. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your information with third parties. We do not share information with advertisers or use it for marketing purposes. Analytics data is processed by PostHog (our analytics provider) and is used solely for understanding site usage.

Third-Party Links

This site contains links to restaurant booking sites (such as Resy, OpenTable, etc.). When you click these links, you will be directed to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or data handling of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to maintain the security of this site. However, no website can guarantee complete security. Given the limited nature of data we collect, the risk is minimal, but we implement appropriate safeguards.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. While we do not anticipate major changes given our simple data practices, we will keep you informed of any updates.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data collection practices, please contact us at hi@nycrsvps.com.
